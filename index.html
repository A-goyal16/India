Indian Players

The Indian National Cricket Team, often referred to as Team India, is under the governance of the Board of Control for Cricket in India. This team holds full membership in the International Cricket Council (ICC), granting them the privilege to participate in Test Matches, One-Day Internationals (ODIs) and Twenty-20 International (T20Is). Team India boasts intense rivalries with several other cricketing nations, with its most notable ones being against Pakistan. Additionally, competitive relationships are cultivated with teams like Australia, South Africa and England...
